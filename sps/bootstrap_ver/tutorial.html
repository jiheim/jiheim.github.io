Start Practicing

Practice as much as you want with the song you want to cover. Adjust the sound effects before recording for practices down to the finest detail.

Project(Song) Settings

Please make sure to edit that the song title and artist as they will be displayed in the video. If there is no video, the album art will be used. Try arranging the images as you want!

Add & Edit Lyrics

Try adding lyrics yourself. If your music has lyrics, they will be added automatically.

Adjust Lyric Sync

Adjust sync to the lyrics if you want to add them to your video. You can sync the lyrics to the desired timestamp of a song. It's ok if you make mistakes! You can always re-adjust the sync by tapping the '^' button.

Fine-tune Lyric Sync

You can precisely adjust the sync to the lyrics.

Record Audio & Video

Tap '+' button to record audio and video.

Adjust Clips Precisely

You can adjust the settings like volume, pitch, auto-volume, EQ, and more when you select the clips.

Trim Clips

If there are repeated or unnecessary clips, try using the 'Trim' feature without having to record again! With the 'Trim' feature, you can easily remove the unwanted parts of the clips for easier recording sessions.

Adjust Clip Sync

If the recorded clip is out of sync with the primary clip, you can adjust the sync precisely down to milliseconds.

Use Video Templates

Select a template that fits your video. You can create awesome video with just one simple selection.

Share to KineMaster

If you want to add more edits to your SingPlay videos, tap the 'KineMaster' button to share them. You can make various edits to your videos.

Change App Language

You can change the app language by going to 'About SingPlay.'

Search Feature

You can now search for the songs or videos you're looking for.
